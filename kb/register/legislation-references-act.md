# Legislation References — Australian Capital Territory

Key ACT legislation for ABE's ACT owner builder and White Card content. Use for course-page legal references and compliance statements.

**Verified:** 26 May 2026 against legislation.act.gov.au and Access Canberra. **Re-verify:** annually. **Confirm section numbers against the current consolidated Act before quoting.**

---

## 1. Building and owner builder

- ***Construction Occupations (Licensing) Act 2004* (ACT)** — a single framework licensing all construction occupations in the Territory (builders, plumbers, electricians, gasfitters, building surveyors), including **owner-builder licences**. Administered by the **Construction Occupations Registrar** (appointed under s103), who sits within **Access Canberra** (part of the Chief Minister, Treasury and Economic Development Directorate). Supported by the **Construction Occupations (Licensing) Regulation 2004** and the **Construction Occupations (Licensing) (Qualifications) Declaration 2024**.
- ***Building Act 2004* (ACT)** — building approvals, inspections and standards; supported by the **Building (General) Regulation 2008** (ACT). The NCC applies through this Act.
- ***Civil Law (Sale of Residential Property) Act 2003* (ACT)** — requires disclosure of building information when selling residential property.
- **Owner-builder specifics:** owner-builder licence issued by the Construction Occupations Registrar **through Access Canberra**; threshold **$12,000**; an **approved owner-builder course** is required; home warranty / residential building insurance applies above $12,000; resale disclosure within **6 years**. Disciplinary and rectification matters go to the **ACT Civil and Administrative Tribunal (ACAT)**.

## 2. WHS / White Card

- ***Work Health and Safety Act 2011* (ACT)** + ***Work Health and Safety Regulation 2011* (ACT)**. Regulator: **WorkSafe ACT**.
- **Construction induction ("White Card"):** the unit **CPCWHS1001** *Prepare to work safely in the construction industry*; the ACT refers to it as the **General Construction Induction Card**. ABE's ACT White Card is delivered by its RTO partner **AlertForce (RTO 91826)**, **face-to-face in a classroom** (self-paced online delivery is restricted to WA and TAS residents).
