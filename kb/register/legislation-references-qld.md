# Legislation References — Queensland

Key QLD legislation for ABE's QLD owner builder and (where referenced) White Card content. Use for course-page legal references and compliance statements.

**Verified:** 26 May 2026 against legislation.qld.gov.au and QBCC. **Re-verify:** annually. **Confirm section numbers against the current consolidated Act before quoting.**

---

## 1. Building and owner builder

- ***Queensland Building and Construction Commission Act 1991* (QBCC Act)** — establishes the **QBCC** as the industry licensing body and regulator; licensing of building contractors and supervisors; dispute resolution and directions to rectify defective/incomplete work; the mandatory **Queensland Home Warranty Scheme**; and the **owner-builder permit system (Division 8 — Owner-builders)**. Supported by the **Queensland Building and Construction Commission Regulation 2018**. Domestic building contracts are governed by **Schedule 1B** of the Act (which absorbed the former Domestic Building Contracts Act).
- ***Building Act 1975* (Qld)** — building work, building development approvals and building certification; supported by the **Building Regulation 2021** (Qld). The NCC applies through this Act.
- **Owner-builder specifics:** permit required for residential work over **$11,000** (total value, incl. GST — labour + materials combined); a **QBCC-approved course** is mandatory before applying; **one permit per 6 years** (exemptions for exceptional circumstances; barred if a permit was cancelled in the last 3 years); a title notation is recorded for **7 years** and resale must be disclosed within **6 years**; the applicant must intend to occupy as principal place of residence.

## 2. WHS / White Card

- ***Work Health and Safety Act 2011* (Qld)** + ***Work Health and Safety Regulation 2011* (Qld)**. Regulator: **Workplace Health and Safety Queensland (WHSQ)**, within the Office of Industrial Relations.
- **Construction induction ("White Card"):** the unit **CPCWHS1001** *Prepare to work safely in the construction industry*. WHSQ refers to the card as the **General Construction Induction Training (GCIT)** card. White Card training must be completed **in person** in QLD (online delivery is restricted to WA and TAS residents).
