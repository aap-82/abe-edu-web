# Legislation References — Tasmania

Key TAS legislation for ABE's TAS owner builder, CPD and (where referenced) White Card content. Use for course-page legal references and compliance statements.

**Verified:** 26 May 2026 against legislation.tas.gov.au and CBOS. **Re-verify:** annually. **Confirm section numbers and regulation years against the current consolidated sources before quoting them.**

> Note: the original backlog hint referenced the *Building Act 2000* — that Act was **replaced by the *Building Act 2016***, which is the current building law in Tasmania.

---

## 1. Building and owner builder

- ***Building Act 2016* (Tas)** (Act No. 25 of 2016) — commenced **1 January 2017**; replaced the *Building Act 2000*. The main building-control law (with the **Building Regulations 2016**); implements the National Construction Code; categorises work as **low-risk / notifiable / permit**. Administered by **CBOS** (the Director of Building Control). Appeals go to the Tasmanian Civil and Administrative Tribunal (TASCAT).
- ***Occupational Licensing Act 2005* (Tas)** — licenses building services providers, **and** is the Act under which an **owner builder holds an owner builder permit**. Supported by the **Occupational Licensing (Building Services Work) Regulations 2016**.
- ***Residential Building Work Contracts and Dispute Resolution Act 2016* (Tas)** — commenced 1 January 2017; applies to residential building and plumbing contracts over **$20,000**; sets minimum contract requirements and a dispute-resolution scheme.
- **Owner-builder specifics:** permit issued under the *Occupational Licensing Act 2005*, administered by CBOS; an **approved Tasmanian owner-builder course** is required (current within **12 months**) and is **not** required for Class 10a/10b structures; the owner builder is liable to subsequent owners for defective building work for up to **10 years** after completion / occupancy permit; applications are lodged at a Service Tasmania shop.

## 2. WHS / White Card

- ***Work Health and Safety Act 2012* (Tas)** — commenced **1 January 2013**; based on the national model WHS laws. The owner builder is the **person conducting a business or undertaking (PCBU)** for WHS purposes. Supported by the **Work Health and Safety Regulations (Tas)** — confirm the current regulation year against legislation.tas.gov.au.
- **Regulator:** WorkSafe Tasmania.
- **Construction induction ("White Card"):** the nationally recognised unit *Prepare to work safely in the construction industry* (**CPCWHS1001** — verified against training.gov.au, 26 May 2026; supersedes and is equivalent to the earlier CPCCWHS1001).

## 3. CPD

- Building-practitioner CPD obligations are administered by CBOS under the *Occupational Licensing Act 2005* framework for licensed building services providers. (Specific CPD point requirements and the approved-activity rules belong in the CPD content, not here.)
