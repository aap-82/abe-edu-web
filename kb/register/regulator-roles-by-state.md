# Regulator Roles by State

Single reference for which Australian regulator administers what in each state ABE operates in, the correct names to use on pages, and ABE's delivery model per course type. Compiled from the skill's canonical State code reference (`page-type-engine.md` §2), `authority-model.md`, `government-listings.md`, and the live ABE and regulator sites.

**Compiled:** 26 May 2026 · **Re-verify:** quarterly (regulator names and department structures change occasionally — see Sources). This is internal reference data; it does **not** contain fees, legislation section numbers, or penalties (those live in their own backlog files).

## 2. Regulator profiles

- **SafeWork NSW** — NSW work health and safety regulator. Administers the general construction induction ("White Card") requirement in NSW.
- **Building Commission NSW** — administers home building regulation under the *Home Building Act 1989*, including owner-builder permits and the CPD criteria for builders and swimming-pool builders. Established 1 December 2023, taking over NSW Fair Trading's building functions (general consumer matters remain with NSW Fair Trading). Owner-builder permit applications are lodged via Service NSW.
- **Workplace Health and Safety Queensland (WHSQ)** — QLD WHS regulator. Administers the QLD construction induction card (the General Construction Induction Training / "GCIT" card).
- **Queensland Building and Construction Commission (QBCC)** — QLD building regulator. Administers owner-builder permits, builder licensing, and builder CPD.
- **WorkSafe WA** — WA WHS regulator. Administers the WA construction induction ("White Card") requirement.
- **Building Services Board** — grants WA owner-builder approvals under the *Building Services (Registration) Act 2011 (WA)*, supported by the **Building and Energy** division within the **Department of Local Government, Industry Regulation and Safety (LGIRS)**. Owner-builder approval is *not* administered by Consumer Protection. WA uses a **knowledge-requirement model** — the applicant declares relevant training/experience on **Form 75** — not an approved-provider register. (LGIRS was formerly DEMIRS — the Department of Energy, Mines, Industry Regulation and Safety — renamed to LGIRS on 1 July 2025; official domain now lgirs.wa.gov.au.)
- **WorkSafe Tasmania** — TAS WHS regulator. Administers the TAS construction induction ("White Card") requirement.
- **CBOS — Consumer, Building and Occupational Services (Tasmania)** — administers TAS owner-builder permits and maintains the approved owner-builder training-provider list, and administers TAS CPD. CBOS does **not** handle White Cards.
- **WorkSafe ACT** — ACT WHS regulator. Administers the ACT construction induction ("White Card") requirement.
- **Access Canberra** — ACT Government shopfront/regulator. Administers owner-builder licences and lists approved owner-builder course providers, and administers ACT CPD.

## 3. Naming rules (apply on every page)

- **SafeWork NSW** — one word "SafeWork", never "Worksafe NSW" or "Work Cover".
- **WHS**, never "WH&S" or "OH&S" (the model WHS laws replaced the OHS terminology).
- WHSQ issues the QLD card as the **General Construction Induction Training (GCIT) card** — the nationally recognised unit is the same, but QLD refers to the card by this name.
- In Tasmania, **CBOS** handles owner builder and CPD; **WorkSafe Tasmania** handles WHS / White Card. Do not attribute White Cards to CBOS.
- WA owner builder: never "Building Services Board-approved provider", "LGIRS-approved provider" or "WA-government-approved provider" — WA uses the Form 75 knowledge-requirement model, so ABE's certificate **supports the Form 75 application / meets WA's training requirement** rather than being "approved".
- NSW builder CPD: never "Building Commission NSW-approved CPD provider", "NSW Fair Trading-approved CPD provider" or "mandated provider" — NSW operates on **self-declaration plus audit**, with no provider-approval regime for builders' CPD. ABE's CPD **meets the NSW CPD criteria** (administered by Building Commission NSW under the *Home Building Act 1989*).
- NSW owner builder: ⛔ **on hold — ABE has no delivering RTO for this course.** The Upskill Institute (RTO 45708) arrangement is unsigned, and the five units NSW requires are not on 45708's scope. Never present ABE as the deliverer or developer of a NSW OB course either. Do not build the page. Canonical status: `kb/rules/authority-model.md` → "NSW Owner Builder".
- ABE is **not** an RTO. Never give ABE an RTO number (the former RTO 91794 was withdrawn). RTO numbers belong only to the partners below.

## 4. ABE's delivery model by course type

- **White Card** — delivered by ABE's RTO partners, never by ABE directly: **Blue Dog Training (RTO 31193)** for QLD, WA and TAS; **AlertForce (RTO 91826)** for ACT; **Upskill Institute (RTO 45708)** for NSW (trainer-led virtual classroom or in person — never self-paced online). Pages must disclose the delivering RTO.
- **Owner Builder** — developed and delivered directly by ABE Education across **QLD, WA, TAS and ACT** (state-approved or state-listed where the regulator maintains such a list — CBOS, Access Canberra — or accepted under a knowledge-requirement model, WA Form 75). **NSW is not currently offered:** the intended Upskill Institute (RTO 45708) arrangement is ⛔ on hold and not in force, and the five required units are not on that RTO's scope. Treat NSW Owner Builder as a product ABE does not have today. Canonical status: `kb/rules/authority-model.md` → "NSW Owner Builder".
- **CPD** — developed and delivered directly by ABE Education where offered (NSW building CPD; TAS building, plumbing and electrical CPD; WA real estate CPD).
