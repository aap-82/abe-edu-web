<Section id="duties" marker="06" eyebrow="Insurance and duties" h2="What insurance and other duties apply?">

But you control the site, so workers compensation and public liability matter, and you carry the licensed builder’s responsibilities, including liability that survives a sale.</AnswerCapsule>
