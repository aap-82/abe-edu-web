<Section id="permit" marker="05" eyebrow="The permit" h2="Do you need an owner builder permit?">
  <AnswerCapsule><b>Yes, if the work is valued over $11,000</b> including GST and it is on your own property. For a genuine farm building the threshold extends to $27,500. The value is what a licensed contractor would charge.</AnswerCapsule>

    canTitle="What a permit lets you do"

  <Note><b>Keep in mind:</b> your Certificate of Completion must be no more than 5 years old on the day you apply. Only one person on a jointly owned title completes the course and becomes the nominated permit holder. Get building approval before any work begins, including earthworks, and you cannot start until the permit is issued. Average QBCC processing is about 30 working days.</Note>
  <Note variant="caution" label="Exemptions"><b>Exemptions:</b> current QBCC licence holders are automatically exempt, as are some qualifying licences and named professionals such as a building surveyor or civil engineer.
