<Section id="permit" marker="05" eyebrow="The permit" h2="Do you need an owner builder permit?">
  <AnswerCapsule><b>Yes, if you are doing a licensed builder’s work</b> on your own property and it is not low-risk work by an owner. For building or extending a home (Class 1a) you must first complete the approved course. It is not required for Class 10a or 10b structures such as sheds, garages and fences.</AnswerCapsule>
